About PlumbEase

Our Founder, Laura Kensington, was a trailblazer in establishing the organized and professional house cleaning industry. Starting her journey in 1981 at the age of 25, Laura quickly made a name for herself with her innovative approach and dedication to excellence. Many prominent companies in the industry sought her guidance and insights when launching their own ventures in the early 80s and 90s.

Laura’s influence on the industry is unmistakable, yet CleanPro Services stands out with its distinctive features that significantly enhance our customer experience. Our commitment to efficiency allows us to offer affordable services without compromising on quality, making CleanPro the preferred choice for many. Laura's vision is encapsulated in our core values, which drive everything we do: Precision, Integrity, and Care.

Laura’s Values Statement of six words—Work diligently. Serve kindly. Live honestly—continues to inspire our team and set the standard for why and how we deliver our services. At CleanPro Services, we are dedicated to maintaining Laura's legacy while continually improving to serve our customers better.

Behind PlumbEase

After the tragic loss of Laura’s husband to cancer at the age of 40, Mark joined the Kensington family as husband and stepfather. Mark brought a fresh perspective and a compassionate spirit to the family and later became involved in the family cleaning business when Corporate needed administrative support. His dedication and organizational skills have been invaluable to CleanPro Services.

The opportunity to travel with Laura to visit service centers across North America has allowed them to ensure that CleanPro maintains the high standards it is known for. Together, they have fostered a culture of excellence and care, which is reflected in every aspect of the company’s operations. Laura and Mark's partnership has strengthened CleanPro’s commitment to providing exceptional service, making the company a leader in the industry.
